Biscuits Recipe

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Peanut Butter
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Water

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and line a baking sheet
  2. In bowl, combine flour and egg
  3. Add peanut butter, water, honey and stir until firm dough
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface
  5. Cut shapes out with a cookie cutter
  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes

Pupcakes Recipe

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Flour
  • Plain Greek Yogurt
  • Egg
  • Apple sauce
  • Baking Soda
  • Oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In bowl, combine flour and baking soda
  3. In another bowl, combine oil, peanut butter and apple sauce
  4. Combine both bowls
  5. Mix in egg
  6. Place batter in baking tins
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes
